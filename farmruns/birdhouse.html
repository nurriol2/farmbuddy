There are 4 places birdhouses can be placed on Fossil Island.

Birdhouses utilize a variety of seeds to capture prey in a birdhouse trap.

Equipment

Equip these items on your character:

  • Full Graceful
  • Digsite Pendant
  • Air staff

Inventory

Check your inventory for these items:

  • Hammer
  • 4 Birdhouses
  • 40 Seeds
  • 1 Law Rune
  • 1 Fire Rune

Seeds

Any of the following seeds can be used with the trap.

  • Barley seed
  • Hammerstone seed
  • Jute seed
  • Yanillian seed
  • Krandorian seed

Steps

  1. Digsite Pendantto Fossil Island
  2. Run North to the teleport
  3. Choose the 2nd option
  4. Replace existing birdhouses
  5. Use the seeds on the birdhouse
  6. Return to the teleport
  7. Choose the 4th option
  8. Replace the North birdhouse
  9. Replace the South birdhouse
