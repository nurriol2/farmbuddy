There are a total of 3 hardwood tree patches in Old School Runescape. They are all located on Fossil Island.

The input cost of one run is currently 86,382 (gp). Given that Mahogany logs net a profit:

Mahogany Plank
Sell: 2061 (gp) each
Sawmill
Fee: 1,500 (gp) each
Total Profit
561 (gp) each

Then, 154 mahogany logs need to be cut down per run to offset the input cost. Cutting this many logs can be done in 5.5 full inventories.

Equipment

Equip these items on your character:

  • Full Graceful
  • Digsite Pendant
  • Dragon Axe

Inventory

Check your inventory for these items:

  • Rake
  • Spade
  • Air Staff
  • 3 Mahogany Sapling
  • 75 Yanillian Hops
  • 1 Law Rune
  • 1 Fire Rune

Steps

  1. Rub the Digsite Pendant to Fossil Island
  2. Run North to the teleport
  3. Choose the 4th option
  4. Run East toward the Hunting icon
  5. Inspect each tree
  6. (Optional) Cut 5.5 full inventories of logs
  7. Right-click or pay to clear the patch
  8. Use the sapling on the patch
  9. Right-click to pay the squirrel
